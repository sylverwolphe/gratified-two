Buttercup Destiny
a gratified project

What is Buttercup Destiny?

Buttercup Destiny is an exploration of fate, choice, and the small moments that shape our paths. Part art installation, part interactive experience, part coffee ritual — it's our most ambitious project yet.

The Concept

01

The Reading

Order a drink. Receive a fortune — not from a cookie, but from a buttercup. Each petal holds a possibility.

02

The Choice

Your fortune isn't set. It's a crossroads. What you do next writes the story.

03

The Archive

Every reading is recorded (anonymously). Watch the collective destiny of our community unfold.
